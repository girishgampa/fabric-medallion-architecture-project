# Business Insights

## Overview

The dashboards provide insights into sales performance, customer behavior, delivery efficiency, and payment trends.

---

## Sales Insights

- Total Revenue
- Monthly Revenue Trends
- Top Selling Categories
- Top Performing Products

---

## Customer Insights

- Highest Purchasing States
- Customer Distribution
- Average Review Score

---

## Delivery Insights

- On-Time Delivery Rate
- Late Deliveries
- Average Delivery Days

---

## Payment Insights

- Most Popular Payment Method
- Installment Trends

---

## Key Findings

- Healthy & Beauty generated the highest revenue.
- Credit cards were the most common payment method.
- Most deliveries were completed on time.
- Customer satisfaction remained above 4/5.
